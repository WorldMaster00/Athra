The First Era.

The First recorded age of man.

Otherwise known as The Golden era, due to its advancements in magic and autonomy. The world relished in its ability to advance. Many great individuals pioneered aspects of life.

Magic, Machines, exploration, industry and much more.

Kingdoms ruled for a hundred years, as is the length of an era.

With no war, no famine, no crime. One hundred years of perpetual, everlasting peace.

But would it last?

This was the most uneventful of times in the world. There was no evil in sight, other than the odd criminal or bandit.

Though this time was a time of peace and quiet, there was much to be recognised and recorded, for advancements of civilisations ranged far and wide.

Irrigation, farming, forgery, ect, as well as the other advancements mentioned above.

Time passed into The Second Era, which brings us to the current era of the world and the present time and day of the roleplay at hand.

YOU Will shape this Era. Your kingdoms, your characters, your choices, your stories.

What part will you play, in shaping this world?
