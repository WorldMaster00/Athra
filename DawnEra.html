The Dawn of true civilisation...

The Dawn of Men. The most prominent race among all... And within the earliest of their days, they knew but nothing, other than war and hatred for one another...

During this time, races sprung up in vast populations across Athra and the wider world.

War was waged constantly and the kingdoms rose and fell...

Nothing noteworthy happened during this time. No vast histories or sprawling empires.

Nothing lasted long enough...

Time passed and survival of the fittest became a truer nation each passing day

Until only the older selves of the current kingdoms remained.

Peace was finally achieved, but at the cost of blood. Enough blood to fill oceans. Much of the land was unused during this time.

Most of it was wild and untamed. Even now...

Old kingdoms survived, while new ones formed and treaties were made. Peace was the priority of most, while others secrelty plotted, but nevertheless...

There was peace...

And that peace was extended way into The First Era.
